How to Use Todo App

Creating Todo Items

To create a new todo item:

  1. Type your task description in the input field on the main page
  2. Press Enter or click the "Add Task" button
  3. Your task will appear in the list below

Marking Items as Complete

To mark a todo item as complete:

  1. Click on the checkbox next to the task
  2. The task will be visually marked as completed
  3. Click again to mark it as incomplete

Deleting Items

To delete a todo item:

  1. Click the delete button (Ã—) next to the task
  2. The task will be permanently removed from your list

Data Storage

Your tasks are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. They will persist between sessions unless you clear your browser data.
